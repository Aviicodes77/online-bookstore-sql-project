# 📊 Key Insights from Online Bookstore Project

## 📚 Sales Insights
- Top-selling books contribute the majority of total sales
- Fiction and Fantasy genres show higher demand compared to others

## 💰 Revenue Insights
- A small number of orders generate a large portion of total revenue
- Monthly revenue trends indicate variation in customer purchasing behavior

## 👤 Customer Insights
- A few customers contribute significantly more revenue than others
- Repeat customers tend to spend more overall

## 📦 Inventory Insights
- Some books have low remaining stock and need restocking
- High-demand books are at risk of going out of stock

## 📈 Trends
- Sales are not evenly distributed across all months
- Certain periods show higher revenue

## 🎯 Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-value customers
- Restock top-selling books regularly
- Promote low-selling books with discounts
